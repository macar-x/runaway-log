## ğŸ” What is RunawayLog?

RunawayLog is a simple, privacy-focused web application that helps you track your desire to escape the daily grind. Every click represents a dream of breaking free, with visual patterns in a calendar heatmap and motivational quotes.

All data stays private in your browser's localStorage, ensuring your information never leaves your device.
